Halodoc Midwives

About The App

Halodoc Midwives is an application designed to assist Indonesian midwives in obtaining all the necessary products and equipment for their practice. Partnering with licensed pharmacies and Gojek, Halodoc Midwives provides access to high-quality products at competitive prices, delivered directly to your doorstep within an hour.

Description

- Develop Halodoc Midwives App (2018-2019)

- Tech Stack : Kotlin, MVP, Retrofit, Room, Push Notification, Google Maps, Payment Gateway (Midtrans), Crashlytics, Unit Test, Multi Modules.
