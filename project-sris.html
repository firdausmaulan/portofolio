Smartfren SRIS

About The App

SRIS APK stands for Smart Retail Information System. This official app from Smartfren is designed for pulse outlets to manage Smartfren services such as top-up, calls, SMS, internet data, transaction history, balance top-ups, and more. For outlet or pulse counter owners, this app also makes it easy for you to activate customers, claim incentives, and purchase credit.
