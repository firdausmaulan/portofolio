Smartfren STS

About The App

A Sales Tracking System is a field-based sales tracking application designed to record daily sales data, calculate sales incentives, generate reports, and provide a geographic information system that integrates with other Smartfren systems. The STS is equipped with a mobile application featuring a built-in GPS tracker.
